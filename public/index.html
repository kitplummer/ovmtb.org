Oro Valley High School Mountain Bike Club

OVMTB is a boys and girls high school mountain bike club representing Ironwood Ridge, Mountain View, Canyon del Oro, and Oro Valley Basis, and fielding teams in the Arizona High School Cycling League race season. We will be working towards supporting middle school programs as well in the future.





As we approach our racing season, we'll be looking for volunteers to help with the Tucson race operations. We're always accepting donations, and discussions regarding team sponsorship and support.

We Are:

...an inclusive club, focusing on the development of young men and women via the sport of mountain bike racing.

It is really that simple. We use mountain biking to bring kids together, enabling them to grow confidence, environmental awareness, fitness, and friendship.

We are part of the Arizona High School Cycling League and the National Interscholastic Cycling Association. We're also strong supporters of the Spirit of Howdy.

The 2013 season has start, and we're training for our first ever race. It will be held, here in Tucson at the Old Tucson Studios on September 29th.

Interested? Don't hesitate to reach out.
