OVMTB is a boys and girls high school mountain bike club representing (but not limited to) Ironwood Ridge, Mountain View, Canyon del Oro, and Oro Valley Basis, and fielding teams in the Arizona High School Cycling League race season. We will be working towards supporting middle school programs as well in the future.




We're always accepting donations, and discussions regarding team sponsorship and support.

We Are:

...an inclusive club, focusing on the development of young men and women via the sport of mountain bike racing.

It is really that simple. We use mountain biking to bring kids together, enabling them to grow confidence, environmental awareness, fitness, and friendship.

We are part of the Arizona High School Cycling League and the National Interscholastic Cycling Association . We're also strong supporters of the Spirit of Howdy.

We are smack dab in the middle of the 2014 high school season, and the team is doing great. Check out our Facebook page all of our results. We're also starting to plan for the fall Stay tuned or contact us for more info.

Interested? Don't hesitate to reach out. The club is open to all high school riders, and middle-schoolers too. The NICA/ASHCL season doesn't provide racing for middle school just yet, but other races are possible.
